About Cruize

Cruze, stands as an iconic marque in the world of motorcycles. Originating in Great India, the brand has a rich legacy of crafting enduring and powerful machines. Known for their distinctive thumping engine note and classic design, Cruze bikes evoke a sense of retro and class while embracing modern engineering. With a steadfast commitment to quality and heritage, Cruze motorcycles continue to captivate riders worldwide, embodying a timeless spirit of exploration and adventure on two wheels.




The Clazzic 350

The Clazzic 350 is an iconic vintage-styled motorcycle loved for its classic cruiser design and dependable performance. It features a 346cc engine, offering a distinct thumping exhaust note. With modern conveniences like electric start, disc brakes, and ABS, it blends nostalgia with practicality. This bike remains a timeless choice for riders seeking a classic look coupled with reliable performance on city roads and highways.

The Everest 411

The Everest 411 is a rugged adventure-touring motorcycle crafted for diverse terrains. Powered by a sturdy 411cc engine, it excels both on and off-road. Its long-travel suspension and upright posture ensure comfort during long rides. With features like knobby tires, luggage mounts, and modern tech such as a digital instrument cluster and dual-channel ABS, the Everest 411 is tailor-made for adventurous exploration across varied landscapes.

The Continental GP 650

The Continental GP 650 by Cruze is a modern classic café racer that combines timeless styling with powerful performance. It's equipped with a robust 648cc parallel-twin engine, delivering a smooth and responsive ride. Embracing its café racer heritage, the bike showcases clip-on handlebars, a sculpted fuel tank, and a distinctive stance. With its agile handling and comfortable riding position, the Continental GP 650 offers an exhilarating experience on both city streets and open roads, appealing to riders seeking a blend of classic aesthetics and dynamic performance.
